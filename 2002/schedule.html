Panel Session: 4:45 - 6:00 pm
Scalable Systems Software: Big Clusters - Big Problems?
Pete Beckman - Director, Turbo Labs
José Moreira - IBM Blue Gene System Software Group
John Kochmar - Pittsburgh Supercomputer Center Advanced Systems Group
Al Geist - Head, DOE Scalable Systems Software Project

Abstract: This interactive panel session will begin with each panelist spending 15 minutes describing their experiences and how they are addressing the problems of systems software for big clusters. They will also express what they considered to be major issues in scalability for the next generation of machines. Audience questions and comments will be encouraged during these position presentations, and the last 20 minutes will be an open Q&A session between the panel and the audience.

Call for Posters ● Cluster 2023

IEEE Cluster 2023 is soliciting submissions from academia, laboratory, and industry professionals to present their latest research findings and work-in-progress in all aspects of cluster, cloud, and grid technologies in the form of posters, which will be included in the conference proceedings. The topics of interest are in the areas of 1) Application, Algorithms, and Libraries, 2) Architecture, Network/Communications, and Management, 3) Programming and System Software, and 4) Data, Storage, and Visualization, as in the Cluster 2023 call for Papers.

Students and young researchers are especially encouraged to submit a poster and present their work in the conference. All submissions will be reviewed by the Posters Program Committee, and the highest-quality student poster will be awarded with the Best Student Poster Award.

Accepted posters will be prominently displayed and presented during a session in the conference. Additionally, posters will be made available on the conference website.

Submissions

The poster submission consists of two separate files:

  1. A 2-page extended abstract (including references) describing the poster content, and the research ideas in the poster. If accepted, this extended abstract will be published in the proceedings of the conference.
  2. A poster draft, in PDF format. IEEE Cluster does not provide an official poster template. The maximum size for research posters is 3' x 4' or 914mm x 1219mm. This fits an A0-sized poster in portrait mode (33.1' x 46.8' or 841mm x 1189mm) or an A1-sized poster (23.4' x 33.1' or 594mm x 841mm) in either portrait or landscape mode.

The 2-page extended abstract should be structured similar to a technical paper, and may not exceed two letter sized (8.5" x 11") pages including figures, tables and references using the IEEE format for conference proceedings (PDF, single-spaced, double column, 10pt text).

Please submit the extended abstract and the poster draft via the online submission system

Important Dates

  • Poster submission deadline: August 5, 2023 August 12, 2023
  • Acceptance Notification: August 24, 2023 August 30, 2023
  • Camera-ready deadline: August 31, 2023 September 11, 2023
  • All deadlines are Anywhere on Earth (AoE)

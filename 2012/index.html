IEEE International Conference on Cluster Computing 2012 (Cluster 2012) is the premier conference on all aspects of cluster computing for computational science. Cluster 2012 is hosted Beijing, China, one of the leading technology cities in China. Attendees will have access to the latest developments in cluster computing technologies and practices. Join the cluster computing community, and discuss new directions, opportunities, and ideas that will influence the future of cluster computing. The conference history can be reachable at http://www.clustercomp.org  including the series of Cluster conferences from 1999-2011.

Scope：

Cluster 2012 welcomes original unpublished paper submissions from researchers in academia, industry, and government, describing innovative research in the field of cluster and high-performance computing.

Major topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Systems Design and Configuration
  • Tools, Systems Software, and Middleware
  • Algorithms, Applications and Performance
  • Storage and File Systems
 
This year's conference will also include Poster, Panel, Workshop, and Tutorial sessions for face-to-face interactions with the technology leaders in cluster computing. Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), CAS, China, hosts with the great honor the Cluster 2012 in Beijing. ICT found the Dawning Company which built the TOP 3 machine at TOP500 last year with collaboration with ICT. ICT was remarked by its multicore CPU product, Loongson.
